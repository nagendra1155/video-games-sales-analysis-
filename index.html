Super Mario Bros

is a popular and iconic video game series created by Nintendo. The series revolves around the adventures of Mario, a plumber, and his brother Luigi, as they embark on various quests to rescue Princess Peach from the primary antagonist, Bowser, the King of the Koopas. The series is known for its engaging gameplay, colorful graphics, memorable characters, and catchy music. Here's a brief description of the Super Mario Bros. video game series

Pokémon Blue Version

the first pair of Pokémon role-playing video games developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo for the Game Boy. These games were originally released in Japan in 1996 as "Pokémon Red" and "Pokémon Green" (Blue was later released as a third version in Japan).

Minecraft

"Minecraft" is a sandbox video game developed and published by Mojang Studios (formerly Mojang AB) and later acquired by Microsoft. Released in 2011, it quickly became a cultural phenomenon and is one of the best-selling video games of all time. Wii Sports

"Wii Sports" is a sports simulation video game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Wii console. It was released as a launch title for the Wii in 2006 and became one of the most iconic and popular games for the console. "Wii Sports" is known for its simple yet engaging gameplay that showcases the Wii's motion-sensing controls. Here's a description of the game

Grand Theft Auto IV

"Grand Theft Auto IV" (GTA IV) is an action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It was released in 2008 for various platforms, including PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, and PC. Set in the fictional Liberty City, a sprawling and detailed open-world environment inspired by New York City, "GTA IV" is the eleventh installment in the highly acclaimed Grand Theft Auto series. Here's a description of the game.

FIFA 16

"FIFA 16" is a sports simulation video game developed by EA Canada and published by Electronic Arts. It is part of the long-running FIFA video game series, which is known for its realistic portrayal of soccer (football) and its focus on both single-player and multiplayer gameplay. "FIFA 16" was released in 2015 for various platforms, including PlayStation 4, Xbox One, PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, and PC. Here's a description of the game.
